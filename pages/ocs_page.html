Medical Atlas

| Age: 68 | Gender: Male | Occ: Minister |

Compared to all Bureaucrats and Civil Servants, Medical is an outlier. An Epideomologist by trade, it is an anomalous phenomena seen in the Bureaucracy. But alas, the system and the way it works had worked for years before, and it still works now so no one was stopping the apparent rise of an Epidemologist among the tight knit ranks of Bureaucrats as long as he fit the bill: pedantic, efficient, amoral, obstructive, and seemingly sleepless. Overtime, the stallion had risen up to Ministerial Secretary for the Minister of Administrative Affairs. And when his predecessor finally passed, it was obvious for the Administration and the Kaisar to appoint him in their steed. And a stallion of such stature is of course, of no ordinary nature. A stallion from a line said to be blessed by the goddess of Administration, it is no wonder his abilities are amazing and effective. With the touch of the divine upon him, only time will reveal upon the rest what machinations he has for his Empire, his people, and his family.
