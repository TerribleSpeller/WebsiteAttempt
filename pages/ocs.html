Medical Atlas

| Age: 68 | Gender: Male | Occ: Minister |

Compared to all Bureaucrats and Civil Servants, Medical is an outlier. An Epideomologist by trade, it is an anomalous phenomena seen in the Bureaucracy. But alas, the system and the way it works had worked for years before, and it still works now so no one was stopping the apparent rise of an Epidemologist among the tight knit ranks of Bureaucrats as long as he fit the bill: pedantic, efficient, amoral, obstructive, and seemingly sleepless. Overtime, the stallion had risen up to Ministerial Secretary for the Minister of Administrative Affairs. And when his predecessor finally passed, it was obvious for the Administration and the Kaisar to appoint him in their steed. And a stallion of such stature is of course, of no ordinary nature. A stallion from a line said to be blessed by the goddess of Administration, it is no wonder his abilities are amazing and effective. With the touch of the divine upon him, only time will reveal upon the rest what machinations he has for his Empire, his people, and his family.

Stylus Pen

| Age: 65 | Gender: Female | Occ: Minister |

Compared to that of Medical Atlas, Stylus Pen was a normal mare. A syndicalist journalist who fell in love with her spotter during her national service, she was soon taken on a wild journey as her husband ascended the ladder of power. At first, she was estatic at the chance of actually implementing reforms through the conduit of her husband - espicially after being thrown out by the unions she once called her family. The continued involement of Medical up the ranks saw Stylus shift eastwards in her ideology towards Social Democracy, abandoning her younger self's anarchistic tendencies. She saw herself rise in fame thanks to the use of her husband's position to break stories of investigative nature, a tad, corrupt method in giving herself fame and power, but it worked as she saw herself offered a position in the NeiBuDanaReviewzburo - and later on, as NeiBuDanaReviewzburofurher, then, Minister of Foreign Affairs. With her power... Stylus knows she can do the right thing. She hopes she can actually do it.
